What is Invadence?

Currently we are working on our first project Invadence. Invadence will be an open-world exploration game set on Mars. The concept is simple, you have crash-landed on the planet with a spaceship and now you have to try to escape. You'll have to collect the parts to repair the spaceship that you crashed. However, this quickly becomes a challenge. You can find more details about this on our YouTube channel.
